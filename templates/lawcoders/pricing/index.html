Fair pricing to solve your
engagement and performance needs

Get started for free regardless of your size. We won't ask you for a credit card or charge hidden fees. After the 14-day trial, all of Teambit is only $6/month for each of your teammates (and free for smaller teams). If you have any questions, talk to us.

Pricing FAQ

  • Can I use Teambit for free?

    Yes, you can use all of Teambit's functionality for free for 14 days. If you'd like we would also personally help to set everything up so that you can see improvements in your team straight away. And if there are 7 or less teammates in your team - you'll enjoy Teambit's forever free plan going forward.

  • How does billing work?

    After 14 days of trial we will send you an email asking to securely connect your payment details. You will be charged $6 for each teammate of your team that month.

  • How do I pay for this?

    We are using Stripe to process your payments. It's the same payment provider that many popular products use. With it you are safe, and we don't have to handle your credit card information directly.

  • Do you have discounts for non-profits?

    We love non-profits! If you are a registered non-profit, reach out to us and you'll be able to use all of Teambit for free with a team of up to 50 teammates and for just $1/month/teammate for larger teams.

  • How long does implementation take?

    You can get started in seconds. Inviting your team is straightforward, we have a bunch of handy predesigned surveys and reviews ready for you to use and we are happy to help you design the best experience for your team.

  • Do you have dedicated support?

    We want to make sure that you have a great experience with Teambit. We are available via live chat, email, and video calls to answer any questions you might have. We can also provide you with a personal consultation for designing surveys or reviews and sharing best practices for rolling Teambit out.

  • How do you approach security?

    We are treating your data seriously. It is hosted on a secure cloud service platform and we do regular penetration testing to make sure that your data is kept safe. For your teammates, we ensure that their data is always private and accessible only to people they shared it with.

  • Can I have a demo before signing up?

    We would love to understand the challenges you are facing and how we can help. You can pick the time that works for you here.

Got more questions? Talk to us.
