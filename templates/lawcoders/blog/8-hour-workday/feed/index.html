]]> By: Jason Haas https://teambit.io/blog/8-hour-workday/#comment-24 Wed, 21 Mar 2018 18:58:00 +0000 http://teambit.io/?p=406#comment-24 I have found that the most productive way to work for me is what I call “binge working”. I am a freelance software developer so I have the flexibility to make my own schedule. Rather than working some kind of arbitrary set hours, I choose a goal for the day and go after it until I finish it. It takes me a couple hours to get into the “flow” work state, but once I am there I want to stay there for as long as I can. I can get more done in that chunk of time (4 – 12 hours), than I can working in smaller, disconnected sprints.
