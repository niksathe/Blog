If you must break the law, do it to seize power: in all other cases observe it. 

Within our homes, our organisations, our communities, and the country we live in, we are surrounded by rules on what is allowed, and what is not. Within a country, these rules are known as the Law, and are some of the most important parts of modern society. From learning about why laws change, to organisational issues such as Alternative Dispute Resolution, Alison's wide selection of free online Law courses will help you increase your understanding of the legal system.
